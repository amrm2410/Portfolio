## Project Overview

Stova is a premium furniture and interior design e-commerce platform that transforms how customers discover, visualize, and purchase modern furniture solutions. The platform combines elegant design with cutting-edge functionality to deliver an exceptional shopping experience.

Featuring expertly crafted furniture collections for kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces, Stova provides customers with personalized design solutions and seamless online shopping. The platform showcases over 500+ premium products with detailed visualizations and expert consultation services.

Built with a focus on comfort, functionality, and timeless design, Stova bridges the gap between traditional furniture retail and modern digital experiences, making high-quality interior design accessible to everyone.

## Project Challenges

### Visual Product Presentation
Creating an immersive visual experience that allows customers to truly appreciate furniture quality and design details through digital interfaces, requiring high-quality imagery and intuitive navigation.

### Design Consultation Integration
Seamlessly integrating personalized design consultation services into the e-commerce platform while maintaining user-friendly interfaces for both customers and design experts.

### Multi-Category Organization
Organizing diverse furniture categories (kitchens, bathrooms, living spaces) in an intuitive way that helps customers easily navigate and discover products across different room types.

## Key Features

- **Premium Furniture Catalog** — Expertly curated furniture collections for kitchens, bathrooms, and living spaces with detailed product information and high-quality imagery
- **Interior Design Solutions** — Personalized design consultation services with expert advice to transform every corner of your home
- **Advanced Product Search** — Intelligent search functionality with filters for categories, price range, style, and room type
- **Responsive Design** — Fully responsive design optimized for all devices, ensuring seamless shopping across desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **E-commerce Integration** — Complete e-commerce functionality with secure payment processing, order management, and customer account features
- **Customer Support** — Integrated customer support system with live chat, contact forms, and comprehensive consultation services

## Wireframes & Structure

The wireframing phase was crucial in establishing the information hierarchy and user flow for the Stova platform. Low-fidelity wireframes helped map out the content structure, navigation patterns, and key interactive elements before moving to high-fidelity designs.

- **Information Architecture** — Organized content hierarchy with clear navigation paths and product categorization
- **User Flow Mapping** — Defined key user journeys from discovery to purchase with optimal conversion paths
- **Responsive Layout Planning** — Planned responsive behavior and mobile-first approach for all screen sizes
- **Component Structure** — Identified reusable components and established consistent design patterns

## UI Design

Modern and elegant homepage design featuring hero section with furniture showcase, product categories, statistics, and comprehensive furniture collections. Key elements include hero section, product showcase, statistics display, and contact form.
